<h2>Reviews</h2>
<pre>"Knives Out is a whole lot of fun, intended to keep the audience off-balance right up until the finish. It's a razor-sharp throwback

"An entertainment that's as smart, witty, stylish and exhilarating as any movie lover could wish for."
